Hello and welcome to my portfolio!

  • La Poste: A mobile app tailored for individual users.
  • La Poste Pro: A solution designed for professional users.

These projects allowed me to enhance my iOS development skills and focus on delivering high-quality, user-centric applications.

In my final year, I took on the role of Mobile Tech Lead, managing the mobile team, mentoring developers, and overseeing project delivery.

My professional journey began at OpenEyesConsulting, where I worked for 1.5 years on several innovative projects, including:

This early experience allowed me to develop a strong foundation in mobile development and understand the diverse needs of clients across different industries.

My approach to iOS development is rooted in a passion for creating efficient, scalable, and user-friendly mobile applications. I am particularly focused on balancing technical excellence with seamless user experiences to deliver impactful solutions.

Throughout my career, I have continuously sought to stay updated with the latest technologies and best practices in mobile development.
